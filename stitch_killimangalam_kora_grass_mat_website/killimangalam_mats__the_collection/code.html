Preserving the Weave

Simple steps to ensure your Kora Grass mat lasts for generations.

air

Dust Regularly

Simply shake the mat or use a soft brush to remove dust from the intricate weave fibers.

water_drop

Avoid Dampness

Keep away from direct water contact. If spilled, wipe immediately and air-dry completely.

sunny

Sun Dry Occasionally

Expose to gentle morning sunlight once a month to maintain fiber flexibility and natural aroma.
