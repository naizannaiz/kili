The Process

The Art of the Looms

01 Harvesting

Sustainably gathering fine kora grass from the lush riverbanks, selected only during peak maturity.

02 Natural Dyeing

Using vibrant, eco-friendly pigments derived from tree bark and medicinal plants to color each strand.

03 Master Weaving

Intricate geometric patterns brought to life on traditional hand-operated looms by master weavers.
